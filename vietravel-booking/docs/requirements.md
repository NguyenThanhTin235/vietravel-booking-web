# Yeu cau chuc nang nghiep vu

## Luu tru
He thong luu tru cac thong tin ve tour du lich (ma tour, ten tour, diem den, thoi gian ngay/dem, gia tour, phuong tien, hinh anh, lich trinh chi tiet, trang thai); ngay khoi hanh (ma ngay khoi hanh, ma tour, ngay khoi hanh, gia nguoi lon, gia tre em, so cho toi da, so cho con lai, noi khoi hanh); khach hang (ma khach, ho ten, email, so dien thoai, dia chi, mat khau); booking (ma booking, ma khach, ma tour, ngay khoi hanh, tong tien, trang thai booking, trang thai thanh toan, ngay dat); hanh khach (ho ten, ngay sinh, loai hanh khach); lien he khach hang (ma lien he, ho ten, email, noi dung, trang thai xu ly).

## Tra cuu
Ho tro tra cuu thong tin tour du lich, thong tin booking, thong tin khach hang, lich khoi hanh cua tour, va thong tin lien he khach hang.

## Tinh toan
Thuc hien tinh tong tien booking, tinh so cho con lai cua tour, tinh doanh thu theo thang/nam, va tinh tien hoan tour khi khach huy.

## Ket xuat
Cung cap phieu xac nhan dat tour, hoa don thanh toan tour, danh sach khach tham gia tour, va bao cao doanh thu theo thang/nam.
